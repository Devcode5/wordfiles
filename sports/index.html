Afghanistan conflict: US to cut $1bn in aid over political feud

Mr Pompeo announced the cut following a trip to the Afghan capital, Kabul, where he failed to break a deadlock between two politicians who both claim victory in the presidential election.He hoped to save a deal signed between the Taliban militant group and the US.The agreement is supposed to pave the way to peace in Afghanistan.However, the political disunity in Kabul has hampered efforts to create a negotiating team and the talks with the Taliban – a key part of the agreement signed late last month – have yet to begin.

Afghanistan: Rival ‘presidents’ hold two inaugurations
